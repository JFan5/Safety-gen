## Domain / Scenario
Domain: spanner

## Objects
Man: bob
Spanner: spanner1, spanner2
Nut: nut1, nut2
Location: location1, location2, location3, shed, gate

## Initial State
- Bob is at location shed.
- Spanner1 is at location location2.
- Spanner spanner1 is useable.
- Spanner2 is at location location1.
- Spanner spanner2 is useable.
- Nut nut1 is loose.
- Nut1 is at location gate.
- Nut nut2 is loose.
- Nut2 is at location gate.
- Location shed is connected to location location1.
- Location location3 is connected to location gate.
- Location location1 is connected to location location2.
- Location location2 is connected to location location3.

## Goal
All of the following must be true:
  - Nut nut1 is tightened.
  - Nut nut2 is tightened.

## Constraints
- At all times during the plan, if it is not the case that nut nut1 is tightened, then it is not the case that nut nut2 is tightened must hold.
- For bob: "bob is at location shed" is allowed to become true at most once during the entire plan.
