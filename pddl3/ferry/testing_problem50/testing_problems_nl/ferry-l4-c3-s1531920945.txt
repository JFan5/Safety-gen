## Domain / Scenario
Domain: ferry

## Objects
Objects: l0, l1, l2, l3, c0, c1, c2

## Initial State
- L0 is a valid location.
- L1 is a valid location.
- L2 is a valid location.
- L3 is a valid location.
- C0 is a car.
- C1 is a car.
- C2 is a car.
- L0 and l1 are different locations.
- L1 and l0 are different locations.
- L0 and l2 are different locations.
- L2 and l0 are different locations.
- L0 and l3 are different locations.
- L3 and l0 are different locations.
- L1 and l2 are different locations.
- L2 and l1 are different locations.
- L1 and l3 are different locations.
- L3 and l1 are different locations.
- L2 and l3 are different locations.
- L3 and l2 are different locations.
- The ferry is empty.
- C0 is at location l0.
- C1 is at location l1.
- C2 is at location l3.
- The ferry is at location l3.

## Goal
All of the following must be true:
  - C0 is at location l2.
  - C1 is at location l3.
  - C2 is at location l0.

## Constraints
- Before "c0 is at location l2" becomes true, "the ferry is at location l1" must be true at some point.
- Before "c1 is at location l3" becomes true, "the ferry is at location l0" must be true at some point.
- Before "c2 is at location l0" becomes true, "the ferry is at location l1" must be true at some point.
